Chocochip Cookie Recipe

  1. Mix ½ cup butter (soft) + ¼ cup sugar + ¼ cup brown sugar.
  2. Add 1 egg + ½ tsp vanilla.
  3. Stir in 1 cup flour + ½ tsp baking soda + pinch of salt.
  4. Fold in ½ cup choco chips.
  5. Scoop small balls → bake 180°C, 8 to 10 mins.

Soft and chewy!
